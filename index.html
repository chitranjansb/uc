Our Workflow

The Art of Creation

Our tried-and-true four-step process ensures transparency, collaboration, and a final product that exceeds every expectation.

01

Conceptual Design & Briefing

We start with a detailed consultation to understand your needs, style, and budget. This phase includes sketching, moodboarding, and material selection.

02

Precision Engineering & Mockups

Our team develops detailed technical drawings and 3D mockups. Once approved, materials are ethically sourced and prepared for the crafting stage.

03

Handcrafting & Finishing

The masterpiece is brought to life. Our artisans meticulously apply traditional Jodhpur techniques, focusing on joinery, carving, and multi-stage finishing.

04

Installation & Final Review

We manage final delivery, professional on-site assembly, and a thorough review to ensure every detail meets our rigorous quality standards and your total satisfaction.
